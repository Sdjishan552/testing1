Honest about how DocStitcher works
Your documents are 100% private — every crop, compress, and export runs entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. No file ever touches our servers. Not even for a millisecond. However, DocStitcher does require an active internet connection to load (we serve ads to cover hosting and keep the app completely free for you). The AI background remover downloads its model once on first use, and individual tools work locally after that — but the app itself needs connectivity to start.

Privacy First

Your documents never leave your device.

DocStitcher processes everything inside your browser using JavaScript and Canvas APIs. No file — not your Aadhaar, PAN card, passport photo, or anything else — is ever sent to any server. The only network traffic is ads (which keep the app free) and the AI model download on first use.

🔒
Zero Server File Uploads All file processing happens using JavaScript and Canvas APIs, entirely in your tab. No backend, no cloud storage, no S3 bucket ever touches your files.
🤖
On-Device AI Model The background removal AI downloads once and runs locally in your browser. Your photos are never sent to any AI service or cloud API.
📢
Why Internet Is Required The app itself and its ads require a live connection to load — this is how we keep DocStitcher free. Once loaded, all your document work stays 100% local and private.
