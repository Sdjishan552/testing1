Frequently Asked Questions
Is DocStitcher really free? ⌄
Yes, completely. Every tool — document stitcher, passport photo maker, background remover, compressor, bulk PDF builder, watermark tool, and image editor — is free with no hidden tiers or limits. Ads keep it running.
Does DocStitcher upload my files to a server? ⌄
No. Everything runs inside your browser using JavaScript. Your Aadhaar, PAN card, photos, or any other file never leaves your device. We have no backend that receives files.
Why does DocStitcher need an internet connection? ⌄
DocStitcher is free because it displays ads. Ads require an internet connection to load. Without ads, there would be no revenue to keep the app free and maintained. We appreciate your understanding.
Which browsers are supported? ⌄
DocStitcher works best on Chrome, Edge, and Firefox (latest versions). Safari on iOS and macOS is also supported. We recommend Chrome for the best experience on Android.
The background removal isn't working well. What can I do? ⌄
The built-in background remover uses an on-device AI model that works best with clear, well-lit photos against a simple background. For complex images, you can enter your own remove.bg API key in the tool settings for much higher accuracy results.
Can I install DocStitcher as an app on my phone? ⌄
Yes! DocStitcher is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On Chrome for Android, tap the three-dot menu and select "Add to Home Screen". On iOS Safari, tap the Share button and choose "Add to Home Screen". Note that an internet connection is still required to use the app.
