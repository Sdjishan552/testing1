Privacy Policy

We built DocStitcher around a simple promise: your files never leave your device. This page explains what that means in detail.

Last updated: June 28, 2026

🛡️
The short version DocStitcher processes everything — cropping, PDF creation, background removal, compression — entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. No file you upload is ever sent to any server, anywhere, except when you personally opt in to the remove.bg background-removal API (see Section 5). We do not store, read, or transmit your documents ourselves.
1Who We Are

DocStitcher ("we", "us", "our") is a free browser-based document utility built and operated in India. The service is accessible at this website and as a Progressive Web App (PWA). For any questions about this policy, contact us at docstitcher@gmail.com.

2Information We Do NOT Collect

By default, the following data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Documents you upload (Aadhaar, PAN, driving licence, passport, photos, etc.)
  • Signatures or watermark images
  • Output PDFs, JPGs, or PNGs you generate
  • Any personal information contained within those files

We have no server-side processing pipeline. There is no backend that receives your files — not even temporarily. The one exception is the optional remove.bg background-removal mode: if you manually enable it and add your own remove.bg API key, the specific photo you run through it is uploaded to remove.bg's servers (a third party, not us) so it can be processed. It is off by default, never activates on its own, and the app confirms with you before the first upload. Full details are in Section 5 below.

3Information We Do Collect

Usage data via Google Analytics (if enabled): We may collect anonymised, aggregated data such as page views, browser type, country, and session duration via Google Analytics. This data cannot be used to identify you personally and contains no document content.

Advertising data via Google AdSense: We display ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to show you relevant advertisements based on your browsing behaviour. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.

LocalStorage: Your remove.bg API key (if you choose to enter one) is stored in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device.

4Cookies

DocStitcher itself does not set any first-party cookies. However, Google AdSense and Google Analytics (third-party services) may set cookies on your device. These are used for ad personalisation and analytics purposes.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or by using a tool like optout.aboutads.info.

5Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — advertising (policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography, served from Google's CDN (policy)
  • remove.bg — optional, opt-in API for AI background removal. This is off by default and is only called if you manually turn it on and enter your own remove.bg API key. When you do, the specific photo you process with it is uploaded to remove.bg's servers for that one operation — no other files, and no automatic or background uploads. DocStitcher shows a confirmation before the first time this happens on your device. (remove.bg's policy)
6Children's Privacy

DocStitcher is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7Data Security

Because all file processing happens locally in your browser, there is no data in transit for us to secure. Your files exist only in your browser's memory while the tab is open and are discarded when you close or refresh it.

8Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of DocStitcher after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
